Notification Alerts

Default alert. This is a neutral notification with no color variant applied.
Informational. This blue alert is used for general information messages.
Error. This red alert indicates a problem that needs attention.
Success. This green alert confirms an action completed successfully.
Warning. This yellow alert highlights something that may need review.
Outline variant. An outlined alert with a full border and tinted background.
Ghost variant. A ghost alert with a transparent background and border only.
